# Local Pricing Model

A pricing model built for a small coastal market. The goal is **not** to price like
a city agency — it is to become the trusted local digital person for businesses that
spend carefully.

The model rests on five principles:

- Lower entry price
- Smaller initial deliverables
- More add-ons
- Affordable monthly care plans
- One premium custom option for serious launches

---

- Brick-and-mortar stores with outdated websites

> "We make sure people can find you, understand what you offer, and contact you from their phone."

- Contact form

- Google Business Profile cleanup

- Service/category cleanup
- Review link setup

**Basic AI-assisted content**

- Simple FAQ section
- 5 review response templates

## Tier 2: Growth & Automation

**Price:** $3,500 starting price — optional range $2,500–$5,000.

This should become the main profit package.

### Best for

- Busy restaurants
- Inns and small lodging businesses
- Vacation rental services
- Contractors
- Cleaning companies
- Property maintenance businesses
- Retail stores that want more repeat customers

### Core promise

> "We help you get more leads, miss fewer calls, and reduce repetitive customer questions."

### Deliverables

**Website upgrade**

- 5-page website or website refresh
- Homepage
- Services/menu/products page
- About page
- FAQ page
- Contact/booking/quote page
- Mobile-first layout
- Stronger calls-to-action
- Review/testimonial section
- Local SEO setup
- Basic analytics

**Local SEO**

- Google Business Profile optimization
- Review request link
- Local keywords
- Service-area or event-related page
- Basic directory cleanup recommendations

**Automation options** — include 2 automations in the base package. Choose from:

1. **Missed call text-back** — sends automatic text after a missed call, captures customer need, helps recover lost leads
2. **Review request system** — sends customers a review link, helps owner get more recent reviews
3. **Simple AI FAQ assistant** — answers basic website questions (hours, location, menu, service area, policies), human handoff for anything complex
4. **Quote request intake form** — collects name, location, job type, photos, urgency, and contact preference
5. **Restaurant/menu update workflow** — helps update menu, specials, Google post, and website content faster
6. **Email list starter system** — collects customer emails, sends simple seasonal promotions

## Important Adjustment: Make Tier 1 Smaller

The earlier Tier 1 had too much inside it. At $995, it should **not** include a large website.

### Good Tier 1 scope

- 1-page website
- Google profile cleanup
- Basic SEO
- Review link
- Simple FAQ
- Contact form

| Review automation       | $250–$750     |
| AI chatbot              | $750–$1,500   |

| E-commerce starter shop | $1,500–$4,000 |

This keeps the entry price low without doing too much unpaid work.

## Recommended "Small Town" Payment Options

For a small market, payment flexibility matters.

**Option 1: Standard**

- 50% deposit
- 50% at launch

**Option 2: Split payment**

- Tier 1: $500 down, then $250/month for 2 months
- Tier 2: $1,500 down, then $750/month for 3 months

**Option 3: Setup + monthly** (good for cautious owners)

- Example — Digital Essentials: $500 setup, then $175/month for 12 months
- Include a contract term so you do not undercharge.

## Final Recommendation

### 1. Digital Essentials — $995

For small local businesses that need the basics fixed.

Includes: 1-page mobile website, Google Business Profile cleanup, basic SEO, contact form,
click-to-call, review link, simple FAQ, 5 social/review templates.

Maintenance: $75–$175/mo

### 2. Growth & Automation — $3,500

For businesses that already have customers but need better follow-up.

Includes: 5-page website or refresh, local SEO, review system, 2 automations,
better contact/quote/booking flow, basic reporting, owner training.

Maintenance: $250–$650/mo

### 3. Startup Launchpad — Custom from $5,000

For new businesses or serious relaunches.

Includes: strategy, website, Google launch setup, local SEO, launch content,
review system, 2–4 automations, training.

Maintenance: $500–$1,250+/mo

## Key Pricing Principle

For Long Beach, the offer should feel like:

> "This is affordable enough to try, useful enough to keep, and practical enough that
> I can understand what I'm paying for."

The premium value should come later through:

- Monthly care
- Seasonal campaigns
- Automation add-ons
- Additional pages
- Review management
- Google visibility work
- Startup/relaunch projects
