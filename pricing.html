Payment options

Flexibility that works for a small market

Pick the structure that fits your cash flow. Every plan starts with a deposit, and totals match the project price you're quoted — no hidden financing fees.

Standard

50% deposit to start, 50% at launch. Simple, and the lowest total cost.

Split payment

Spread the project cost over a few months:

  • Digital Essentials: $500 down, then balance over 2 months
  • Growth & Automation: $1,500 down, then balance over 3 months
  • Startup Launchpad: custom milestone schedule across the launch

Setup + monthly

Lower upfront cost on a contract term — e.g. Digital Essentials at $500 setup + $175/mo for 12 months. Good when you'd rather keep the starting price small.
